# Design System: ABP Capital

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

The design exudes corporate confidence through its restrained color palette and generous whitespace. The interplay between deep blues and clean whites creates a sense of financial stability and trustworthiness. Light typography weights soften the corporate edge, making complex financial services feel approachable and human.

Subtle elevation is achieved through soft shadows on content cards and the hero section. The design uses minimal layering with 2-3 depth levels, creating gentle separation without overwhelming the clean aesthetic.

Clean rectangular forms dominate with minimal corner rounding. The architectural photography in the hero reinforces geometric precision. Icons and interactive elements maintain sharp, professional edges aligned with the corporate aesthetic.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

**Primary Foundation:**
- Trust Blue (#007cba) — primary
- Deep Navy (#004a59) — secondary

**Accent & Interactive:**
- Professional Blue (#006ba1) — accent

**Neutral & Surface:**
- Clean White (#ffffff) — text
- Corporate Black (#000000) — text

**Structural:**
- Subtle Silver (#cacbcc) — border

## 3. Typography Rules

**aauxproot** (custom), **fontello** (custom)

- **heading-2**: aauxproot 45px/50px, weight 300, letter-spacing: 0.4px — Section headings
- **heading-3**: aauxproot 30px/36px, weight 300, letter-spacing: -0.3px — Sub-section headings
- **body**: aauxproot 18px/26px, weight 400 — Base body text
- **inline-text**: aauxproot 18px/26px, weight 400 — Inline text elements
- **link**: aauxproot 18px/26px, weight 400 — Hyperlinks
- **button**: Arial 13.3333px/normal, weight 400 — Button labels

## 4. Component Stylings

* **hero-card:** Large white content card with subtle shadow, featuring red accent text for headlines and structured content layout with generous padding
* **service-tile:** Dark blue background tiles with white text, clean typography hierarchy, and minimal styling for service category presentation
* **property-card:** Image-based cards with price overlays, clean white backgrounds, and subtle borders for real estate listings
* **news-card:** Light gray background cards with structured content layout, consistent spacing, and subtle visual separation
* **text-link:** Clean text links with red accent color, no underlines, maintaining readability and brand consistency

## 5. Layout Principles

Content follows a structured grid with consistent vertical rhythm. Generous margins and padding create breathing room, while the centered layout maintains focus. Section spacing uses the defined scale to create clear content hierarchy.
